One Indian Girl

One Indian Girl is a novel by the Indian author Chetan Bhagat. The book is about a girl named Radhika Mehta, who is a worker at the distressed debt group of Goldman Sachs, an investment bank.

The Girl in Room 105

The Girl in Room 105 is the eighth novel and the tenth book overall written by the Indian author Chetan Bhagat. The book became a bestseller based on prearranged sales alone. It tells about an IIT coaching class tutor who goes to wish his ex-girlfriend on her birthday and finds her murdered.

Eleven Minutes

Eleven Minutes is a 2003 novel by Brazilian novelist Paulo Coelho that recounts the experiences of a young Brazilian prostitute and her journey to self-realisation through sexual experience.

The Spy

In The Spy by Paulo Coelho, dancer Mata Hari rebels against the accepted roles of women for her time period in order to follow her dreams. At the beginning of World War I, she is sought by German officials to act as a spy because she is having an affair with the man who will become France's minister of war.
